Storyworld

Inner planets

  • Start of civilization.
  • Society of consumers and rich people.
  • Harder to change their social structures.
  • Represented by a president.
  • They have simple recycling programs.

Outer planets

  • Settlers from the inner planets (hippie community, eco-pirates).
  • Unstable political system, no official leader, but several gangs.
  • Mix of outlaws, migrants, space pirates, people with no other place.
  • Need for recycling. Not because they want to, but because they have to survive.
  • They know the problems of the inner system and are proud to not be like them.
